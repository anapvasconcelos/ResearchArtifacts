ROSEICSE 2020

  • About
  • Program

The ICSE’20 ROSE festival celebrates open science artifacts in software engineering. “Science” needs a community committed to critiquing and improving each others’ work. Increasingly, SE is becoming more of a science as more and more papers offer research artifacts as part of their results. The ROSE festival rewards the authors of the better artifacts. So come along and join the festival. Learn how your own work can benefit from artifacts shared by other researchers.
